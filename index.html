Overview

SECOND CHANCE is a platform designed to help previously incarcerated individuals and felons reintegrate into society. The platform provides access to educational resources, job training, and mental health support. It fosters a supportive community by connecting users to mentors, local services, and peer networks, helping them rebuild their lives with confidence and stability.

Objectives

  • Education: Knowledge is power so in order to make any reasonable amount of change, we need to educate the masses. This includes basic life skills and job skills.
  • Outreach: Guidance is necessary for improvement. The system is complicated so it helps when you have people who can help you navigate.
  • Jobs: One of our goals is to make each person self-sufficient. Job listings that are applicable to each person’s situation and skills can be the difference between sustained employment and reentering the system.
