Overview

SECOND CHANCE is a platform designed to help previously incarcerated individuals and felons reintegrate into society. The platform provides access to educational resources, job training, and mental health support. It fosters a supportive community by connecting users to mentors, local services, and peer networks, helping them rebuild their lives with confidence and stability.

Objectives

  • Education
    Knowledge is power so in order to make any reasonable amount of change, we need to educate the masses. This includes basic life skills and job skills. This is the most important part and goal of our project.
  • Outreach
    Guidance is necessary for improvement. The system is complicated so it helps when you have people who can help you navigate. In order to effectively build a new life, people need community and guidance. This is an important part to support our goal.
